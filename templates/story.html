Project Story

Electricity demand is increasing globally due to industrial growth, urbanization, and technological advancement.

Using Tableau, we identified key patterns such as peak consumption seasons, high-demand sectors, and future growth projections.

Insights from this analysis can support policy-making, energy planning, and sustainable development.
